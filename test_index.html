About Us

Sri Lanka’s paddy farmers have long been the backbone of the nation’s food security. However, they often face unstable markets and inefficient systems.

GrainChain is a centralized digital platform that connects farmers, distributors, and government officials to streamline paddy management.

  • Ensure fair pricing for farmers.
  • Streamline supply chains and reduce inefficiencies.
  • Strengthen national food security.

Our Mission

GrainChain aims to digitally transform Sri Lanka’s rice industry through:

  • Farmers: Enabling fair trade and timely payments.
  • Officials: Providing real-time analytics for better decision-making.
  • Distributors: Enhancing logistics to ensure timely deliveries.

How It Works

GrainChain makes paddy management efficient and transparent. Here’s how:

  1. Registration: Field officers input verified farmer data.
  2. Collection: Paddy is gathered and stored in regional warehouses.
  3. Monitoring: Real-time analytics help officials manage supply.
  4. Distribution: Efficient release prevents shortages and stabilizes pricing.
  5. Fair Trade: Payments are processed promptly with full transparency.

Our Vision

We envision a Sri Lanka where every grain is valued and every farmer is empowered. Our goals include:

  • Enhancing transparency and fair pricing.
